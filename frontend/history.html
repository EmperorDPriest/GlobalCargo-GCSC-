The Beginning
A Vision Forged
in Old England

The story of Global Cargo Shipping Company begins in the autumn of 1990, in a modest freight office on the outskirts of London. Founded by a team of determined logistics professionals who believed that international shipping could be done with greater transparency, care, and reliability than the industry standard of the time, GCSC was built on a simple but powerful promise: every shipment, treated as if it were our own.

In those early years, the company carved a reputation across the United Kingdom and Europe for its personal service, meticulous customs knowledge, and the kind of accountability that larger competitors simply couldn't match. Small businesses, manufacturers, and even private individuals chose GCSC not because it was the cheapest, but because it was the best.

By the mid-1990s, GCSC had established freight corridors across Western Europe, expanded its air freight capabilities, and signed its first transatlantic partnership agreements — setting the stage for the company's defining chapter.

GCSC operations at the dawn of the company — precision and care from day one.
Our Journey
35 Years of Milestones
1990
Founded in London, England
Global Cargo Shipping Company is established at Canary Wharf, London, by founder Richard H. Ashworth and a team of five freight specialists. The company's first shipment — a consignment of industrial components — departs from Heathrow Airport to Hamburg, Germany. First-year revenue exceeds projections by 40%.
1994
European Network Established
GCSC opens offices in Frankfurt, Amsterdam, and Paris, creating one of the most comprehensive freight forwarding networks in Northern Europe. The company surpasses 5,000 shipments per year and launches its first dedicated customs brokerage division. ISO 9001 certification achieved.
1998
First Transatlantic Corridor
GCSC launches its inaugural direct transatlantic freight service between London Heathrow and JFK International, New York. The route, combining air and sea options, immediately becomes the company's highest-performing trade lane. A strategic partnership with a leading US customs house positions GCSC as a credible transatlantic player.
2003
US Headquarters Opens — New York
Recognizing the extraordinary growth potential of the North American market, GCSC establishes its United States headquarters at One World Trade Center, New York City. The company registers as an FMC-licensed NVOCC and begins building its domestic US freight network. The transatlantic becomes the company's primary revenue corridor within 18 months.
2008
Weathering the Storm — Growth Through Crisis
While the global financial crisis devastated many logistics firms, GCSC's conservative financial management and diversified client base allowed the company not only to survive, but to acquire three struggling regional US freight companies at favorable valuations. By 2010, GCSC had offices in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, and Miami — becoming a true national US freight brand.
2014
Technology Revolution — Live Tracking Platform
GCSC launches its proprietary shipment tracking platform, giving customers real-time visibility into every stage of their cargo's journey. The platform, built over three years by an in-house engineering team in New York, processes over 2 million tracking events per day by 2016. GCSC becomes one of the first mid-tier US freight forwarder to offer live GPS tracking as standard.
2018
100 Countries. 100 Reasons to Trust.
GCSC achieves a landmark milestone — active shipping operations in 100 countries. The company is awarded the US Department of Commerce Export Achievement Award, recognizing its role in helping American small businesses access global markets. Annual US revenue exceeds $400 million for the first time. The London office, now a European regional hub, celebrates 28 years of uninterrupted operation.
2021
Smart Logistics — AI-Powered Operations
GCSC invests $85 million in its Smart Logistics initiative — deploying AI-powered route optimization, automated warehousing robots, and predictive customs clearance technology across its US network. The COVID-19 supply chain crisis, far from breaking GCSC, validates its investment in redundancy and technology. GCSC becomes a critical lifeline for US healthcare and pharmaceutical supply chains during the pandemic.
2025
35 Years Strong — America's Freight Partner
Today, Global Cargo Shipping Company operates from 14 US regional offices, serves 198+ countries, and employs over 4,800 professionals worldwide. Voted America's Best Freight Forwarder for three consecutive years, GCSC continues to honor the values instilled in a small London office in 1990: reliability, transparency, and the unwavering belief that every shipment matters. The best chapter is yet to be written.
