Green Logistics

Shipping That Protects Our Planet

The freight industry is responsible for approximately 8% of global carbon emissions. At GCSC, we're taking measurable, accountable steps to reduce our environmental footprint — and help our clients do the same. Our 2035 Net-Zero Roadmap commits us to science-based targets across every operation.

🌿
Carbon Offset Program
Every GCSC shipment has a calculated carbon footprint. We offer verified offset options through Gold Standard certified projects.
⚡
Electric Fleet Transition
50% of our US urban delivery fleet will be electric by 2027. We're partnering with leading EV manufacturers to accelerate the transition.
🌊
Green Ocean Shipping
Partnering with carriers using LNG, methanol, and ammonia fuel alternatives. Green ocean freight available on all major trade lanes.
♻️
Zero-Waste Warehouses
All 14 US GCSC facilities are targeting zero-landfill status by 2026. 100% renewable electricity in our warehouses since 2023.
📊
Client CO₂ Reporting
Business account clients receive detailed scope 3 emissions reports for all GCSC shipments, ready for ESG and sustainability filings.
🌱
Tree Planting Initiative
GCSC plants one tree for every 10 shipments processed. Over 2.1 million trees planted to date across reforestation projects.
