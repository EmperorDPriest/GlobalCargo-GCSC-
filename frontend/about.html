Our Mission

Moving the World Forward

Global Cargo Shipping Company was founded in London in 1990 with a simple mission: make international shipping accessible, reliable, and transparent for businesses of every size. Thirty-five years later, that mission drives everything we do — from a single parcel to a full container fleet.

🎯 Our Mission

To be the most trusted international freight partner for US businesses — delivering with precision, honesty, and care across 198+ countries every single day.

👁️ Our Vision

A world where every business — regardless of size — can access world-class global logistics at fair, transparent prices, backed by technology and human expertise.

💡 Our Values

Integrity first. Customer-obsessed. Technology-powered. Sustainability-minded. We hold these values in every shipment, every interaction, and every decision we make.

🏆 Our Awards

America's Best Freight Forwarder (2023, 2024, 2025), Top 50 US Logistics Companies, FMC Outstanding Compliance Award, and C-TPAT Tier 3 Certified Partner.
